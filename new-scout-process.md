## Getting Started

Welcome! Here are the first steps to join our troop:

1. Complete the [Youth Application (PDF)](https://filestore.scouting.org/filestore/pdf/524-406.pdf) -- include your Scout ID if transferring from another unit
2. Complete the [New Scout Contact Form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdiUJ2SldEZ-V8PFVZoXZJ8zuscC0eyQZ102C7Ifq2mPfswhA/viewform)
3. Meet with the Membership Coordinator
4. Meet the Scoutmaster(s)
5. Meet with the Treasurer

## Required Enrollment Forms

The following forms must be submitted as part of enrollment:

1. **Medical Forms A & B** ([Download PDF](https://troops258and358.org/uploads/1/3/6/1/136157342/680-001_abc.pdf)) -- Part C is required for summer camp
2. **Front & back copy of medical insurance card**
3. **Code of Conduct Agreement** ([Download PDF](https://troops258and358.org/uploads/1/3/6/1/136157342/troop_258_358_code_of_conduct_with_techchip_form.pdf))
4. **Parental Commitment to Emergency Transport** ([Download PDF](https://troops258and358.org/uploads/1/3/6/1/136157342/troop_258_358_parental_commitment_to_transport_local_campout_form.pdf))
5. **About My Scout Form** ([Download PDF](https://troops258and358.org/uploads/1/3/6/1/136157342/form_005_about_my_scout_for_troop_258___358__fillable_form.pdf))
6. **Permission to carry Epi-Pen / Asthma Inhaler** ([Download PDF](https://www.bsacac.org/my_files/epi_pen_and_inhalers_cac.pdf)) -- *only if applicable*
